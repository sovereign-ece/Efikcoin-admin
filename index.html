📄 Whitepaper: EfikCoin Eternal

1. Vision

To create a self‑sustaining, decentralized financial ecosystem that provides every human with access to banking, savings, and investment tools – free from intermediaries and censorship.

2. Tokenomics (ECE)

Total Supply: 100,000,000 ECE (fixed, no minting after initial).
Distribution: 40% Public Sale & Liquidity, 20% Staking Rewards, 15% Ecosystem Development, 10% Team (3‑year vesting), 10% Marketing, 5% Airdrops & Referrals.
Deflationary & Liquidity Mechanism: 2% of every transaction is automatically sent to the ECE/BNB liquidity pool, providing continuous price support and rewarding long‑term holders.

3. Technology

Smart contract deployed on BSC (address: 0x9F8C29E496ECB6C39c221458f211234DfCB233E0). Verified on BscScan. Non‑custodial wallet with encrypted backups. All transactions include a 2% fee that flows directly into the liquidity pool.

4. Roadmap

  • Phase 1 (Complete): Token deployment, wallet launch, staking UI.
  • Phase 2 (Q2 2025): Real staking contract, lending/borrowing protocol.
  • Phase 3 (Q3 2025): Cross‑chain bridge (BSC ↔ Ethereum ↔ Polygon).
  • Phase 4 (Q4 2025): DAO governance, fiat on‑ramp integration.
  • Phase 5 (2026): Mobile app, EfikCoin debit card.

5. Security & Audits

Smart contract fully audited by Team Finance (pre‑deployment). Ongoing third‑party audits planned every 6 months. Wallet encryption uses industry‑standard AES‑256 via ethers.js. No admin keys can steal user funds – non‑custodial by design.

6. Sustainability & Future Generations

EfikCoin is built to last. A portion of all protocol fees will be directed to an “Eternal Fund” – a multi‑sig wallet used for ecosystem grants, disaster recovery, and educational programs for children. Our goal is to create a financial legacy that outlives us all.
